Meet the team

Jacqueline Gong


Jacqueline Gong, founder and CEO of Soji Financial, Inc., is a licensed CPA in Massachusetts with over 10 years of extensive background in tax compliance and business accounting.

During her time at public accounting firms like PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), Jacqueline has seen a wide variety of work. She has also spent time at Manulife (previously known as John Hancock), a publicly traded company, in both their investment management and accounting divisions.

She brings her industry expertise in tax return preparation for individuals, partnerships, and corporations, along with accounting services for nonprofits, private, and public companies.

Jacqueline received both her Bachelors of Science degree in Management and Accounting and her Masters of Science degree in Finance from the University of Massachusetts Boston. In her spare time, she likes to play basketball, snowboard, and explore different styles of dance.
